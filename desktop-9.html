The water supply situation in Kirti Nagar has become dire, with irregular and insufficient supply posing significant challenges. Basic needs like drinking water and sanitation are not being adequately met, exacerbating health risks. The scarcity has disrupted daily routines and impacted businesses, calling for urgent action from authorities to address the issue and ensure the well-being of the community.

 

In addition to health and economic concerns, the water scarcity in Kirti Nagar has also led to social tensions within the community. Competition for limited water resources has sometimes resulted in conflicts among residents, further highlighting the urgent need for a sustainable solution to the water supply issue.
